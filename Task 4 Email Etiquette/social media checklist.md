
# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## Do’s
- Use respectful and professional language  
- Keep your profile clean and updated  
- Think before posting anything online  
- Share useful, positive, and informative content  
- Respect others' opinions and privacy  

## Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information  
- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content  
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation  
- Avoid engaging in online arguments or conflicts  
- Do not use abusive or disrespectful language
